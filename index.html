Hey there! I'm Lindsey. While I've only been in Fort Worth for 10 years, I feel like this is and has always been my home. I moved to Fort Worth from Northeastern Pennsylvania in 2016. Scranton, to be exact... any Office fans?! I decided in my mid-twenties that snow was just not my thing. I wanted sunshine and a good time. I set my eyes on Texas, got a job, and never went back.

I feel like once you hit the 10-year mark, you’re considered a local. At least by my standards anyway. Besides, what’s the point of living somewhere and calling it home if you can’t call yourself a local?! I love this city with all my heart. It’s got the heart and charm of a small town nestled in a huge metropolitan area. I am an active person. I love hiking, paddleboarding, running (kinda), roller-skating, playing tennis, pickleball, and the list goes on. I’m outside, okay? Like… a lot. It's my favorite thing. My second favorite thing? Eating. Everything. All the time. I love food, and Fort Worth has it all. From pasta to tacos to thai and even espresso martinis and girl dinner!

So, while gatekeeping my favorite spots sounds like a good idea, I can’t do that to y’all. It took me FOREVER to navigate this place when I first moved here, so I’ve decided to break it down into categories, and then I’ll tag the general area of the city they are in. You can choose a place to have fun, shop, and eat and know you’re not accidentally driving across town at rush hour on a Friday. Have you seen 35?!

Hopefully these places that are near and dear to my heart will inspire you to explore the city and find your own faves.

Happy Adventures!
xo, Lindsey
